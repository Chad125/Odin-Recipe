Potato Latkes

Patato Latkes also known as potato pancakes with a side of apple sauce, I know apple sauce as a side sounds super weird but trust me you wont want it any other way after trying it. The apple sauce adds the perfect amount of sweetness and really ties this dish together. This recipe makes 6 servings.

Ingredients

  • 4 Large Potatos
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 2-4 Table Spoons of all purpose flour (depending on desired thickness)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Vegitable Oil
  • Apple Sauce or Sour Cream (I highly recommend apple sauce)

Making the Potato Latkes

  1. Grate the potatos and drain any extra liquid.
  2. Mix together the egg, flour, salt and pepper, once mixed add grated potatos and mix until it all sticks together.
  3. If you want a thicker texture add more flour, if not skip this step.
  4. Make mounds of your stuck together potato mixture and drop them into hot oil, flatten with spatula and cook until golden brown.
  5. Plate with side of apple sauce and or sour cream, Enjoy!
